Sports are games or physical activities people do for fun or competition.  
Common sports include football, cricket, basketball, and tennis.  
People play sports to stay healthy, build teamwork, and enjoy time with others.  
Some sports are played indoors, like table tennis. Others are played outdoors, like soccer.  
Athletes train hard to improve their skills and win matches.  
Watching sports is also fun for many fans around the world.  
Sports bring people together and teach discipline, patience, and teamwork.
