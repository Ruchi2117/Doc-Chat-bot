Fruits are the edible, seed-bearing parts of flowering plants. They are an essential part of a healthy diet, rich in vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants.

Apples come in many varieties and are high in dietary fiber and vitamin C. Bananas are rich in potassium and vitamin B6, and they provide quick energy. Oranges are known for their high vitamin C content and are popular as fresh fruit and juice.

Grapes can be red, green, or purple and are rich in antioxidants like resveratrol. Berries such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries are nutrient-dense and packed with fiber and vitamin C.

Mangoes are tropical fruits known as the "king of fruits" in India, rich in vitamin A and beta-carotene. Watermelons are refreshing summer fruits that are 92% water and help with hydration.

Fruits are often classified into categories like citrus fruits, stone fruits (peaches, plums), berries, and melons. Eating a variety of fruits every day helps in improving immunity, digestion, and overall well-being.
